Electric Vehicle Subsidy

The Victorian Government offers a subsidy of up to $3,000 for the purchase of a new zero-emission vehicle (ZEV), including both battery electric vehicles (BEVs) and hydrogen fuel cell electric vehicles (FCEVs).

  • Eligibility: Available for individuals, businesses, and non-profits. Must purchase a new ZEV priced under $68,740 (including GST).
  • Apply directly through the authorized dealer.

Victorian EV Charging Program

The Victorian Government provides grants to support the installation of fast charging stations across the state. This aims to reduce range anxiety and accelerate EV adoption with infrastructure investments.

Stamp Duty Discount

Electric and hydrogen vehicles in Victoria are exempt from luxury car tax (LCT) and benefit from a stamp duty discount, reducing costs at the time of registration.

  • Learn More Here
Electric Vehicle Road User Charge Exemption

Victoria has implemented a temporary exemption from road user charges for fully electric vehicles to compensate for their exemption from fuel excise.

  • Learn More Here
Solar Homes Program (For EV Chargers)

Victoria’s Solar Homes Program offers rebates for the installation of solar panels and battery storage. This can help EV owners charge their vehicles more sustainably and reduce energy costs.

  • Eligibility: Victorian homeowners can apply for rebates on solar panel installation and battery storage systems, reducing household energy costs while supporting EV charging.
  • Learn More Here
Victorian Government Fleet Policy

The Victorian Government is committed to converting its fleet to zero-emission vehicles by 2030. This includes bulk purchasing incentives and policies for businesses and local governments to encourage widespread EV adoption.
